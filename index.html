What is RoastCard?


RoastCard provides you with an intuitive web interface where you can effortlessly create your very own coffee roasting log tailored to your tastes and needs.


But that's not all! We also offer a range of templates for quick and easy reference, perfect for those eager to embark on their roasting adventure.


With RoastCard, you can easily print or save your logs on your computer, ensuring that your roasting knowledge is always at your fingertips for future use and modifications.

RoastCard is your gateway to a world of coffee roasting perfection. Whether you're a seasoned coffee artisan or just getting started, our customizable coffee roasting log application is here to cater to your unique style and preferences. With RoastCard, you have the power to design and document your coffee roasting journey like never before.

Get Started with RoastCard

Expert Guidance for Creating the Perfect Log

X

Design Your Roasting Log

  • 1. Choose an Item:
    • Select an item from the widget bar on the left side of the screen.
    • Drag and drop the chosen item onto the canvas area on the right side of the screen.
  • 2. Visual Guidance:
    • The canvas area will change color:
    • Green indicates that the item can be dropped.
    • Red signals that your pointer is outside of the drop area.
  • 3. Free Movement:
    • Move items freely within the canvas area to customize your roasting log.

Modifying your Items

  • 1. Choose an Item:
    • Select an item from your canvas and double-click it.
    • An editing window will appear with options for the selected item.
  • 2. Edit Your Item:
    • Each item has its own customization choices.
    • Explore different options to tailor the item to your preferences.
    • Once you've finished customizing, click "Apply" to save your changes.
    • If you no longer want the item, select the trash bin icon to remove it from your Roast Log.

Manage Your RoastCard

  • 1. Saving
    • Click the "Save" button in the top right to save your RoastCard.
    • You will be prompted to enter a name for the card upon saving.
  • 2. Loading
    • To load a previously saved RoastCard, click the "Load" button in the top right.
    • Select the RoastCard from your saved directory.
    • Click "Open," and your card will be loaded onto the canvas.
  • 3. Printing
    • Select the "Print" button in the top right to open the print preview page for your card.
    • Click the "Print" button to print your RoastCard.
  • 4. Reset Canvas
    • Select the "Reset" button in the top right to clear all items from your canvas.
